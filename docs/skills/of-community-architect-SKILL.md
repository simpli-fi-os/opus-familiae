# Opus Familiae Community Architect

> *"Normalization through community is the actual product."*
> (From the Simpli-FI Thesis)

## Skill Overview

This skill designs, builds, and optimizes the Opus Familiae community: the membership platform, accountability structures, referral networks, conversion funnels, and member experience that turn content consumers into committed families on the Save/Invest/Earn path. The community is where OF families stay after the content hooks them. It is the retention engine, the referral engine, and the normalization engine.

The core insight from the Simpli-FI Thesis applies directly: escape velocity from financial dysfunction requires immersive proximity to people who have already normalized the target behaviors. A family trying to save 40% of their income needs to be surrounded by families who already do it. That is what the OF community provides. Not information (the internet has plenty). Proximity.

## Community Architecture

### The Conversion Funnel

```
Free Content (YouTube, Podcast, Social)
    ↓ (Lead Magnet: Family Freedom Sprint Blueprint)
Email Subscriber
    ↓ (Welcome Sequence: 5 emails, 10 days)
Engaged Subscriber
    ↓ (Community Launch Invitation)
Paid Community Member ($19-$49/mo)
    ↓ (Course Enrollment)
Course Student ($197-$497)
    ↓ (Invest Pillar Readiness)
SFA Referral (SEC-compliant solicitor agreement)
    ↓ (Success + Story)
Ambassador (earns commissions, creates content, normalizes for next family)
```

Every stage has a specific trigger, a specific value proposition, and a specific next step. The funnel is not linear for every family. Some will jump from subscriber to course buyer. Others will sit in the community for a year before buying a course. The architecture accommodates both.

### Member Tiers

**Tier 1: Free (Content Consumer)**
- Access to all YouTube, podcast, and blog content
- Email newsletter
- Social media community (comments, replies)
- Value: Education, inspiration, awareness

**Tier 2: Community Member ($19-$49/month)**
- Everything in Free, plus:
- Accountability pod assignment (4-6 families per pod)
- Monthly live Q&A with Hunter and Lindsey
- Exclusive content (detailed breakdowns, member-only videos)
- Team of Experts directory access (TSI-powered, OF-filtered)
- Community forum/feed
- Value: Accountability, connection, access to vetted professionals

**Tier 3: Course Student (one-time $197-$497 per course)**
- Access to structured course content (Family Freedom Sprint, Invest Playbook, OF Creator Toolkit)
- Course-specific cohort community
- Worksheets, templates, calculators
- Value: Structured education, implementation frameworks

**Tier 4: Ambassador (application-based, earned)**
- Everything in Community Member, plus:
- Referral commission structure
- Early access to new content and courses
- Featured in OF content (member spotlight, case study)
- Direct line to Hunter and Lindsey for content collaboration
- Value: Income, recognition, deeper involvement in the mission

### Pricing Strategy

**Founding Member Pricing (first 100 members):** $19/month
- Creates urgency and rewards early adopters
- Lower barrier to entry while community is small
- Locked rate for life (or for 12 months minimum)

**Standard Pricing (after founding cohort):** $49/month
- Reflects the value of accountability pods, live access, and Team of Experts
- Annual option: $399/year (saves ~$189, improves retention)

**Course Pricing:**
- Family Freedom Sprint (Save pillar): $197 introductory, $297 standard
- Passive Income Playbook (Invest pillar): $297-$497 (higher price reflects higher-value content)
- OF Creator Toolkit (Earn pillar): $297-$497

**Bundling:** Community + one course at a discount. Never discount the community below $19/mo. The price anchors the commitment.

## Accountability Pods

Pods are the core community structure. They are where normalization happens.

### Pod Design

- **Size:** 4-6 families per pod. Small enough for real relationships. Large enough for diverse perspectives.
- **Duration:** 90-day cohort cycles. Pods reform each quarter. Members can stay with the same pod or shuffle.
- **Meeting rhythm:** Weekly check-in (async in community platform) + biweekly video call (30 minutes).
- **Structure per check-in:**
  1. Win of the week (one financial or family win, however small)
  2. Number of the week (one metric: savings rate, debt paid, income earned)
  3. Challenge of the week (one thing that's hard right now)
  4. Commitment for next week (one specific action)

### Pod Leader Model

- Experienced OF members (6+ months in community) can apply to become Pod Leaders
- Pod Leaders facilitate check-ins, keep the pod accountable, escalate issues
- Pod Leaders receive: free community membership, priority course access, recognition
- Pod Leaders are NOT financial advisors, therapists, or coaches. They are accountability partners with experience. Clear boundaries.

### Pod Matching Criteria

Match families based on:
1. **Stage:** Where they are in Save/Invest/Earn (don't mix a family at $200K debt with one that's debt-free)
2. **Family structure:** Similar ages of children, similar household dynamics
3. **Income bracket:** Rough alignment so financial advice feels relevant
4. **Geography (optional):** Same time zone at minimum. Same city is a bonus for potential in-person meetups.

## Team of Experts Referral Network

The Team of Experts is Opus Familiae's version of a family office advisory team, delivered at scale through TSI's vetted provider directory.

### How It Works

1. **TSI collects and vets providers** across seven functional areas (Financial Planner, Investment Advisor, Tax Strategist, Estate Planner, Insurance Specialist, Career & Creator Coach, Family Life Educator).
2. **OF applies an additional filter:** providers who serve the OF community must understand the OF mission, serve families at OF-typical income and asset levels, and agree to OF's service standards.
3. **Community members access the filtered directory** through their membership dashboard. Search by function, location, specialty, and availability.
4. **Referral tracking:** Every connection between an OF member and a Team of Experts provider is tracked. This enables referral fee collection (where applicable and compliant) and quality monitoring.

### Integration with Simpli-FI Alpha

The Investment Advisor function is handled directly by Simpli-FI Alpha LLC. This referral is governed by a formal SEC-compliant solicitor agreement. Key compliance requirements:

- The solicitor agreement must be in writing and disclosed in Alpha's Form ADV
- OF must not provide specific investment advice or make performance guarantees
- Referral fees are permissible under SEC rules if properly disclosed
- All referral communications must include appropriate disclaimers

**Template disclaimer for content referencing SFA:**
"Simpli-FI Alpha LLC is a registered investment adviser. Opus Familiae receives referral fees for qualified introductions. Past performance does not guarantee future results. Investment involves risk of loss. See Simpli-FI Alpha's Form ADV for full disclosures."

### Provider Onboarding

When onboarding a new Team of Experts provider:
1. Verify credentials through TSI's standard vetting process
2. Confirm alignment with OF mission and service standards
3. Execute provider agreement (service level expectations, referral fee terms if applicable)
4. Create provider profile in TSI directory with OF tag
5. Introduce provider to community through member-facing content

## Community Events

### Recurring Events

| Event | Frequency | Format | Purpose |
|-------|-----------|--------|---------|
| Live Q&A with Hunter & Lindsey | Monthly | Video call, 60 min | Direct access, builds trust, answers real questions |
| Pod Check-ins | Biweekly | Video call, 30 min | Accountability, connection, normalization |
| Expert Spotlight | Monthly | Guest video, 45 min | One Team of Experts provider presents on their area |
| Debt Payoff Celebration | As earned | Community post + shout-out | Celebrate families hitting milestones |
| Quarterly Challenge | Quarterly | 30-day structured challenge | "No-Spend January," "Side Income March," etc. |
| Annual Summit | Yearly (Phase 3+) | In-person or virtual event | Keynotes, workshops, networking, milestone awards |

### Community Rituals

Rituals create belonging. Design them intentionally.

- **First Friday Numbers:** On the first Friday of each month, every member posts their key number (total debt remaining, savings rate, or monthly side income). No judgment. Just numbers. Normalization in action.
- **Win Wall:** Dedicated space where members post wins, however small. Paid off a credit card. Had a date night. Started the YouTube channel. The community celebrates everything.
- **Milestone Badges:** Visual markers for achievements. Debt-free badge. First $10K invested badge. First $1K earned badge. Five-year member badge.

## Platform Selection Criteria

When evaluating Circle vs. Skool vs. custom build:

**Must-haves:**
- Community feed/forum with threaded discussions
- Course hosting (or easy integration with course platform)
- Member directory with profiles
- Event scheduling and video call integration
- Payment processing (Stripe preferred)
- API access for TSI integration and metrics tracking
- Mobile app (most OF members will be on phones)

**Nice-to-haves:**
- Gamification (points, levels, badges)
- Pod/group management built in
- Referral tracking
- Analytics dashboard
- White-label branding

**Evaluation framework:**
1. Can it support accountability pods natively or with minimal workaround?
2. Does it integrate with Stripe for payment flexibility?
3. Can we connect it to TSI's API for the Team of Experts directory?
4. Does it feel warm and personal, or corporate and cold?
5. What does it cost at 100 members? At 500? At 1,500?

## The Normalization Flywheel

This is the fundamental mechanism that makes the OF community work:

```
Family enters OF community
    ↓
Encounters families who have already normalized the target behaviors
(aggressive saving, intentional scheduling, debt elimination, creator income)
    ↓
Begins to internalize: "This is normal. People like me do this."
    ↓
Achieves their own milestones
    ↓
Becomes the normalization agent for the next family entering the community
    ↓
Flywheel accelerates
```

Every community design decision should be tested against this flywheel. Does this feature make it easier for a new family to see that the target behaviors are normal? Does this feature make it easier for an experienced family to share their story? If the answer to both is yes, build it.

## Launch Sequence

### Pre-Launch (Months 1-6, during Phase 1 content creation)
- Build email list to 1,000+ subscribers
- Tease community in content ("We're building something for families like us")
- Identify 10-15 founding families from email list and social engagement
- Select and configure platform
- Design onboarding flow
- Create first month of exclusive content

### Soft Launch (Month 6-7)
- Invite founding families at $19/mo
- Run first accountability pod cycle (90 days)
- Host first live Q&A
- Collect feedback aggressively
- Iterate on pod structure, content mix, and platform UX

### Public Launch (Month 8-9)
- Open community to all email subscribers
- Standard pricing: $49/mo
- Launch ambassador program for founding members
- Begin Team of Experts provider onboarding through TSI
- First Expert Spotlight event

### Scale (Month 12+)
- Launch first course (Family Freedom Sprint)
- Formalize SFA solicitor agreement
- Expand pod leader program
- Begin quarterly challenges
- Target: 100 members by month 12, 500 by month 24
